The most interesting engineering decisions a Series-A team makes aren't the
features — they're the systems that make next quarter's features cheap.

## Three boring bets that pay off

1. **Observability before you need it.** A dashboard built calmly beats one
   built at 3am.
2. **Deploys you trust.** If shipping is scary, you ship less, and the team
   compounds caution.
3. **One source of truth per concept.** Two ways to define a customer is a
   future incident.
