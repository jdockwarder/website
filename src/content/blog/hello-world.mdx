Small teams move fastest when every member can punch above their weight. AI
tooling is the cheat code, but only if the work still feels like *yours*.

## Where leverage actually shows up

Most teams reach for AI to write code. The bigger gain is upstream — turning a
fuzzy idea into a brief that survives contact with the codebase.

<Callout client:load title="Try this">
  Start your week by writing the **smallest** plan that lets a teammate execute
  without asking you anything. AI can draft this for you in minutes; the act of
  reviewing it is what sharpens the thinking.
</Callout>

The interesting work isn't the tokens — it's the editing.

## A quick checklist

- Codify the *why* before the *how*.
- Reject any AI output you wouldn't be proud to ship.
- Keep humans on the boundary: review, taste, ownership.
